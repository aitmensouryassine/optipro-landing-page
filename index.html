About

Behind Every Great Project
A Great Story

The inspiration for our optician store management app, OptiPro, is deeply rooted in a personal journey that began with a simple conversation between my partner, Hajar, and me, Yassine. It all started when Hajar shared the excitement that her sister was on the verge of realizing her dream of becoming an optician and opening her own store.

As Hajar passionately spoke about her sister's aspirations, we couldn't help but recognize the potential challenges that lie ahead in managing an optician business effectively. The complexities of order management, customer interactions, and inventory control were apparent, and the desire to support Hajar's sister became a shared vision between us.

In that moment, Hajar proposed a powerful idea – why not create a tool that could simplify the management processes for her sister's future optician store? It was an epiphany that resonated deeply with both of us. The prospect of developing a solution that could empower aspiring opticians to focus on their craft rather than getting bogged down by administrative tasks was inspiring.

Motivated by the shared goal of helping Hajar's sister succeed in her venture, we embarked on the journey to build OptiPro. Our vision was to create an app that not only streamlines the intricacies of optician store management but also becomes an indispensable companion for anyone navigating the challenges of running a successful optical business.

OptiPro is more than just a software solution; it's a testament to the passion for supporting small businesses and the dreams of aspiring opticians like Hajar's sister. We hope that OptiPro becomes the catalyst for success, enabling opticians to focus on what they do best – providing exceptional service and care to their customers.

Hajar El Abdellaoui

Yassine Ait Mensour
